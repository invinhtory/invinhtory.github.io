Hi, I'm Vinh! I am a PhD student at the Integrated Fabrication Laboratory in Mechanical Engineering at the University of Washington, advised by Professor Corie L. Cobb.

Currently, my research focuses on developing additive manufacturing (AM) workflows for printing Lithium-ion batteries and integrating them directly into printed electronics. More broadly, I am interested in eveloping novel fabrication workflows to unlock new functional and creative possibilities in device design. I work at the intersection of additive manufacturing (AM), materials science, and computer-aided design (CAD) systems.

Feel free to see more about what I do and get in touch!
