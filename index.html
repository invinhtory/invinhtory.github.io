Hi, I'm Vinh! I am a PhD student at the Integrated Fabrication Laboratory in Mechanical Engineering at the University of Washington, advised by Professor Corie L. Cobb.

My research centers on developing novel fabrication workflows to unlock new functional and creative possibilities device design. I work at the intersection of additive manufacturing (AM), materials science, and computer-aided design (CAD) systems. Currently, I am working on AM of Lithium-ion batteries and integrating them into printed electronics. I have also worked previously on fabrication workflows for nano-wire bundles

I also work on some personal projects that explore the creative side of fabrication and science. Feel free to check them out!
